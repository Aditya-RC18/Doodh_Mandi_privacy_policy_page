01

Information We Collect

When you register and use Doodh Mandi, we collect the following information:

  • Full name
  • Mobile phone number
  • Shop name or business name
  • Order data (items, quantities, amounts, payment method)
  • Device token (FCM token) for push notification delivery
We do not collect your location, contacts, camera, microphone, or any other sensitive device data.
02

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Create and manage your supplier or retailer account
  • Process and display your daily milk orders
  • Send push notifications about new orders, order reminders, and auto-order confirmations
  • Enable payment processing via Cash, UPI, or Razorpay
  • Improve the performance and reliability of the app
03

Data Storage

All data is securely stored on Supabase, a cloud database platform with industry-standard encryption. Your data is protected using secure HTTPS connections at all times.

04

Push Notifications

Doodh Mandi uses Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) by Google to send push notifications to your device. Your device token is stored in our database solely for this purpose.

You can disable notifications at any time through your device settings.

05

Payment Information

Payments made via UPI or Razorpay are processed by the respective third-party payment providers. Doodh Mandi does not store your payment card details, UPI PIN, or any sensitive financial information on our servers.

06

Sharing of Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. Your data may be shared only in the following limited cases:

  • With Firebase (Google) for push notification delivery
  • With Razorpay for payment processing
  • With Supabase for secure data storage
  • When required by Indian law or court order
07

Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active. If you request account deletion, your personal data will be removed from our systems within 30 days of the request.

08

Account Deletion

You have the right to request deletion of your account and all associated data. You can do this from within the app using the Delete Account option in your profile settings, or by contacting us at the email address below.

Upon deletion, your name, phone number, shop name, FCM token, and order history will be permanently removed.

09

Children's Privacy

Doodh Mandi is a B2B business application intended for adults (18 years and above). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware that a child under 18 has provided us with personal information, we will delete it immediately.

10

Security

We take the security of your data seriously. All data is transmitted over HTTPS, stored with encryption, and access is restricted to authorised systems only.

However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

11

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted within the app and on the Play Store listing. Continued use of the app after changes means you accept the updated policy.
